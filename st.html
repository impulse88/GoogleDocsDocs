Creating a 2-Variable Line Chart
Google Sheets makes it easy to plot linear or roughly linear relationships between 2 variables.
1.
Enter the data as shown below, using a column to store all data points for each variable and giving the first cell of each column a variable label that will become an axis label in the generated graph. The first column should store the independent variable's data points.
2.
Highlight the data you entered by clicking the first cell, keeping the mouse button held down, and dragging down and to the right until both columns are fully highlighted.
3.
Click "Insert" on the menu bar, then click "Chart...". The chart creation menu will appear.
4.
In the "Chart editor" menu that appears on the right, click the field below "Chart type", then scroll down until you see a graph icon with 2 straight lines and click it. Your chart will appear on the spreadsheet.
